The more we focus outside, the more unconscious we are of our ‘discomforts’ or how we are feeling. This Divine BEing that is you is always present and you can access it in many ways. If you are like most of us and desire to finally be ‘seen’ and develop Intimacy (into-me-see) with another in your life then take the time to SEE yourself first. This will allow you to BE seen and to haveintimacy with others because you will BE seen within first.
